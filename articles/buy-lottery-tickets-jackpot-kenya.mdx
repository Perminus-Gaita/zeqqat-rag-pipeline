## How to Buy SportPesa Jackpot Tickets {/*section:sportpesa-tickets*/}

{/* Context: Three methods: Mobile app (download, register, deposit, pick), SMS (send MJP#predictions to 79079 at KSh 99), USSD (dial */}

### Method 1: Mobile App (Easiest)

**Step 1:** Download SportPesa app (Google Play or Apple App Store)

**Step 2:** Register with your phone number

**Step 3:** Deposit via M-Pesa:
- Click "Deposit"
- Enter amount (minimum KSh 50)
- Confirm M-Pesa prompt
- Funds appear instantly

**Step 4:** Select jackpot (Mega or Midweek)

**Step 5:** Make your predictions (1=Home, X=Draw, 2=Away)

**Step 6:** Use double chance if desired (costs multiply by 2 per game)

**Step 7:** Confirm and submit (KSh 99 per entry)

---

### Method 2: SMS (No App Needed)

**Format:** `MJP#1X22222XXXX11XX12`

**Breakdown:**
- `MJP` = Mega Jackpot
- `#` = Separator
- Then 17 predictions: `1` (Home), `X` (Draw), `2` (Away)

**Example:**
```
MJP#1X12X221X2X1X2X12
```

**Send to:** 79079

**Cost:** KSh 99 deducted from M-Pesa automatically

**Confirmation:** You'll receive SMS confirmation with slip number

---

### Method 3: USSD (Feature Phones)

**Dial:** *790#

**Follow prompts:**
1. Select "Jackpot"
2. Choose Mega or Midweek
3. Enter predictions
4. Confirm M-Pesa payment

**Perfect for:** Users without smartphones

## Double Chance Explained {/*section:double-chance*/}

{/* Context: Cover 2 outcomes per game. Each double chance doubles total stake. 3 doubles = 8 combinations at KSh 792. Use only when  */}

**What it is:** Covering 2 outcomes on a single game

**Example:**
- Normal bet: Pick "1" (Home) = 1 combination
- Double chance: Pick "1X" (Home or Draw) = 2 combinations

**Cost impact:**
- Each double chance game **doubles** your total stake
- 3 double chance games = 8 total combinations (2^3)
- 5 double chance games = 32 total combinations (2^5)

**When to use:** Only on games where two outcomes have similar probability (e.g., odds within 0.40 of each other)

Learn more: [How to Increase Your Jackpot Chances](/i/increase-chances-winning-sportpesa-jackpot)

## Common Mistakes When Buying Tickets {/*section:common-mistakes*/}

{/* Context: Five mistakes: not checking slip before submitting, too many double chance picks, buying random slips, forgetting to che */}

### Mistake 1: Not Checking Slip Before Submitting
Always review your picks. Once submitted, you can't change them.

### Mistake 2: Using Too Many Double Chance Picks
Using 10 double chance on a 17-game slip costs KSh 101,376. Most bettors should use 3-5 maximum.

### Mistake 3: Buying Multiple Random Slips
Better to research ONE quality slip than buy 10 random ones.

### Mistake 4: Forgetting to Check Results
Set reminders! Many winners discover they won days later.

### Mistake 5: Not Saving Slip Number
Always screenshot or write down your slip number for verification.
