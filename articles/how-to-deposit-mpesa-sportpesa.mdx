## Method 1: Via App (Easiest) {/*section:via-app*/}

{/* Context: Open SportPesa app, click Deposit, enter amount (min KSh 50), confirm M-Pesa prompt. Funds appear instantly. */}

**Step 1:** Open SportPesa app

**Step 2:** Click "Deposit"

**Step 3:** Enter amount (minimum KSh 50)

**Step 4:** Confirm M-Pesa prompt on your phone

**Step 5:** Funds appear instantly

## Method 2: Via M-Pesa Menu {/*section:via-mpesa-menu*/}

{/* Context: Go to M-Pesa > Lipa na M-Pesa > Paybill. Paybill number 955100, account is your phone number, minimum KSh 50. */}

**Step 1:** Go to M-Pesa menu

**Step 2:** Select "Lipa na M-Pesa"

**Step 3:** Select "Paybill"

**Step 4:** Enter:
- Paybill: **955100**
- Account: **Your phone number**
- Amount: Minimum KSh 50

**Step 5:** Enter M-Pesa PIN

**Step 6:** Check SportPesa balance (updated in 1-2 minutes)

## Troubleshooting Failed Deposits {/*section:troubleshooting*/}

{/* Context: If money deducted but not in SportPesa: wait 5 min, check M-Pesa transaction ID, contact support. Refund within 24 hours */}

**Issue:** Money deducted but not in SportPesa account

**Solution:**
1. Wait 5 minutes
2. Check M-Pesa message for transaction ID
3. Contact SportPesa support with transaction ID
4. Refund processed within 24 hours
