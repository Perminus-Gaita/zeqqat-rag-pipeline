## Basic Requirements {/*section:basic-requirements*/}

{/* Context: Five requirements: age 18+, Kenyan resident, valid Kenyan ID or passport, Kenyan mobile number, M-Pesa account. */}

✅ **Age:** 18 years or older
✅ **Residency:** Kenyan resident (citizen or legal resident)
✅ **ID:** Valid Kenyan ID or Passport
✅ **Phone:** Kenyan mobile number (Safaricom, Airtel, Telkom)
✅ **Payment:** M-Pesa account

## Age Verification {/*section:age-verification*/}

{/* Context: SportPesa requires ID number during registration, photo of ID for KYC, and date of birth verification. Under 18 cannot p */}

SportPesa requires:
- ID number during registration
- Photo of ID for KYC verification
- Date of birth verification

**Under 18?** You cannot play. Anyone caught using fake IDs risks permanent ban.

## Residency Rules {/*section:residency-rules*/}

{/* Context: Kenyan citizens and legal residents can play. Tourists, non-residents without Kenyan ID, and people in unlicensed countr */}

**Who CAN play:**
✅ Kenyan citizens
✅ Legal residents with valid permits
✅ Anyone with Kenyan ID

**Who CANNOT play:**
❌ Tourists (short-term visitors)
❌ Non-residents without Kenyan ID
❌ People in countries where SportPesa isn't licensed

## Multiple Accounts {/*section:multiple-accounts*/}

{/* Context: One person = one account. Multiple accounts detected via ID verification, permanently closed, winnings forfeited. */}

**Not allowed.** One person = one account. Multiple accounts will be:
- Detected via ID verification
- Closed permanently
- Winnings forfeited

## Self-Exclusion {/*section:self-exclusion*/}

{/* Context: SportPesa offers voluntary self-exclusion for 6 months to permanent. Contact support or visit Responsible Gaming Kenya. */}

If you have gambling problems:
- SportPesa offers self-exclusion
- Contact support to voluntarily ban yourself
- Period: 6 months to permanent
