### 1. Squad Rotation
Teams playing Thursday/Sunday often rest key players midweek.

**Impact:** More upsets, less predictable results

### 2. Motivation Varies
Teams fighting relegation prioritize weekend games over midweek cups.

**Impact:** Check what's at stake for each team

### 3. Lower Leagues Featured
Midweek often includes Championship, Serie B, Ligue 2.

**Impact:** Higher draw rates (28-31% vs 23-27% in top leagues)
