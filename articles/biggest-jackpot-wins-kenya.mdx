**Date:** August 3, 2025  
**Amount:** KSh 424,600,000

## The 7-Year Gap (2018-2025) {/*section:what-it-takes*/}

After Cosmas Korir won in September 2018, **NO ONE won the SportPesa Mega Jackpot for nearly 7 years** until Bonnie Kamau in August 2025.

**Why the gap?**
- Extreme difficulty (1 in 129 million odds)
- Many weeks with 0 correct 17/17 predictions
- Prize pool accumulated to record levels
- Bonnie's win broke the longest drought in jackpot history

**Lesson:** Winning requires immense patience. Average winners play 3-5 years before their first win.

## Tax Changes: Before vs After Finance Act 2025 {/*section:dream-vs-reality*/}

{/* Context: Dream: win this week and quit job. Reality: average winner played 3-5 years, continued working 6-12 months post-win, bon */}

### Old System (Pre-July 2025)
**20% tax on net winnings**

Example (KSh 200M win):
- Winnings: KSh 200,000,000
- Stake: KSh 99
- Net winnings: KSh 199,999,901
- Tax (20%): KSh 39,999,980
- **Take-home: KSh 160,000,020**

### New System (Post-July 2025)
**5% tax on ALL withdrawals**

Example (KSh 200M win):
- Winnings: KSh 200,000,000
- Withdrawal tax (5%): KSh 10,000,000
- **Take-home: KSh 190,000,000**

**Impact on Bonnie Kamau:**
- Under old system: Would have paid KSh 85M tax
- Under new system: Paid KSh 21.2M tax
- **Saved: KSh 63.8 million** due to timing
