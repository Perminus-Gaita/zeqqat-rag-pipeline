## When to Use Double Chance {/*section:when-to-use*/}

{/* Context: Good: two outcomes have similar probability (e.g., Home 38%, Draw 32%). Bad: one outcome heavily favored (e.g., Home 72% */}

**GOOD:** Two outcomes have similar probability
- Example: Everton vs Brighton (Home 38%, Draw 32%, Away 30%)
- Use: Home/Draw double chance (covers 70%)

**BAD:** One outcome is heavily favored
- Example: Man City vs Bournemouth (Home 72%, Draw 18%, Away 10%)
- Use: Single Home pick (save money)
