## AI's Role in Jackpot Research {/*section:ai-role*/}

{/* Context: AI helps with research but cannot predict match outcomes reliably. */}

AI can help with **research** but can't predict match outcomes reliably.

## What AI CAN Do {/*section:what-ai-can-do*/}

{/* Context: Summarize team news, compile H2H records, identify form patterns, suggest matches to research deeper, calculate probabil */}

✅ Summarize team news quickly
✅ Compile head-to-head records
✅ Identify form patterns
✅ Suggest matches to research deeper
✅ Calculate probabilities based on odds

## What AI CANNOT Do {/*section:what-ai-cannot*/}

{/* Context: Cannot predict exact match results, access real-time data without plugins, hack bookmaker systems, or guarantee wins. Be */}

❌ Predict exact match results
❌ Access real-time data (without plugins)
❌ "Hack" bookmaker systems
❌ Guarantee wins

## Best AI accuracy: 50-56% per game {/*section:how-to-use*/}

{/* Context: Example prompt: 'Analyze Arsenal vs Chelsea based on last 5 games, H2H, home/away form.' AI provides data summary for yo */}

Even top AI models achieve only 50-56% accuracy, same as disciplined human research.

## How to Use AI Effectively

**Prompt example:**
"Analyze Arsenal vs Chelsea fixture based on last 5 games for each team, head-to-head record, and home/away form."

**AI provides:** Summary of data for YOU to interpret.
