## What Is the SportPesa Jackpot? {/*section:what-is-jackpot*/}

{/* Context: This is the introductory section of the Ultimate SportPesa Jackpot Guide, explaining the basic format and history of the jackpot. */}

The SportPesa Jackpot is a football prediction game where you predict the outcomes of 13 (Midweek) or 17 (Mega) football matches. Each match has three possible outcomes: Home Win, Draw, or Away Win. Get all predictions correct and you win the jackpot. Get close and you win a bonus.

The minimum stake is **KSh 99**. The jackpot prize pool is shared if multiple people win. As of February 2026, the SportPesa Mega Jackpot has produced only **5 winners in history**, with the largest payout being **KSh 424.6 million** won by Bonnie Kamau in August 2025.

## How to Play {/*section:how-to-play*/}

{/* Context: This section of the Ultimate SportPesa Jackpot Guide covers the step-by-step process of placing a jackpot bet via SMS, app, or website. */}

### Playing via SMS

Send your predictions to **79079** in the format: `MJP#1X22222XXXX11XX12` (for Mega Jackpot). The cost is automatically deducted from your M-Pesa. Learn more in our [complete ticket buying guide](/i/buy-lottery-tickets-jackpot-kenya).

### Playing via App

Download the SportPesa app, register, deposit via M-Pesa, and make your picks. Full tutorial available in our [apps guide](/i/best-apps-jackpot-results-kenya).

{/* Context: This section of the Ultimate SportPesa Jackpot Guide breaks down the mathematical probability of winning the Mega and Midweek jackpots. */}

The mathematical probability of winning the Mega Jackpot is **1 in 129,140,163**. The Midweek Jackpot is **1 in 1,594,323** — still extremely unlikely, but 81 times better than the Mega.

**Key draw indicators:**
- Teams with similar Expected Goals (xG) ratings
- Mid-table clashes with low motivation
- Matches with tight head-to-head records

## What to Do If You Win {/*section:what-to-do-if-you-win*/}

{/* Context: This section of the Ultimate SportPesa Jackpot Guide covers tax obligations and financial planning for jackpot winners in Kenya. */}

Winning is just the beginning. Tax, investment, and financial planning are critical. Under Kenya's Finance Act 2025, you pay a **5% withholding tax** on all withdrawals from your betting wallet (down from the previous 20% on net winnings).
