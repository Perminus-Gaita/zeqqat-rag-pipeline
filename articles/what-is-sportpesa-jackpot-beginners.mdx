## SportPesa Jackpot in Simple Terms {/*section:simple-terms*/}

{/* Context: One-line explanation: predict 13-17 football match outcomes correctly to win millions. */}

**Predict the outcomes of 13-17 football matches. Get all correct = win millions.**

## Two Types {/*section:two-types*/}

{/* Context: Mega Jackpot: 17 games, KSh 99, prize KSh 100-400M, odds 1 in 129M. Midweek: 13 games, KSh 99, prize KSh 10-20M, odds 1  */}

**Mega Jackpot:**
- 17 games
- Cost: KSh 99
- Prize: KSh 100-400M
- Difficulty: Very hard (1 in 129M)

**Midweek Jackpot:**
- 13 games
- Cost: KSh 99
- Prize: KSh 10-20M
- Difficulty: Hard (1 in 1.6M)

## How It Works {/*section:how-it-works*/}

{/* Context: Five-step process: SportPesa announces fixtures, you predict Home/Draw/Away for each, matches play, results announced, c */}

**Step 1:** SportPesa announces fixtures (usually Premier League, La Liga, Serie A)

**Step 2:** You predict each match: Home Win (1), Draw (X), or Away Win (2)

**Step 3:** Matches play over the weekend

**Step 4:** SportPesa announces winners

**Step 5:** Collect winnings (if you won)

## What You Can Win {/*section:what-you-can-win*/}

**Grand Prize:** Get 17/17 correct = Full jackpot

**Bonus Tiers:**
