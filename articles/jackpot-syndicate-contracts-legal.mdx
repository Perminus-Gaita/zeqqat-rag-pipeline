## Legal Status of Syndicates {/*section:legal-status*/}

{/* Context: Betting syndicates are legal in Kenya but contracts must be properly structured to be enforceable. */}

Betting syndicates are **legal in Kenya** but contracts must be properly structured.

## Essential Contract Elements {/*section:essential-elements*/}

{/* Context: Must include: all members' names and ID numbers, contribution amounts, profit split formula, designated bettor, dispute  */}

**Must include:**
1. All members' names and ID numbers
2. Contribution amounts
3. Profit split formula
4. Who places bets (designated bettor)
5. Dispute resolution process
6. Exit conditions

## Enforcement {/*section:enforcement*/}

{/* Context: Verbal agreements are hard to enforce. Always use written, signed contracts witnessed by 2+ people. */}

Verbal agreements are hard to enforce. Always use **written, signed contracts** witnessed by 2+ people.
